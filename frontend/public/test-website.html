Support Articles

Shipping Policy
Standard shipping within the United States takes 5-7 business days and is free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat shipping fee of $5.99. Express shipping is available at checkout for an additional $14.99 and delivers within 2 business days. We currently ship to all 50 US states and Canada. Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number by email within 24 hours. Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days are processed the same day. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.
Return & Refund Policy
We accept refunds within 30 days of purchase for any item in its original, unworn condition with tags still attached. To start a refund, contact our support team with your order number and the reason for return. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. Shipping costs for the original order are non-refundable, and customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or defective. Gift cards and digital products are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances.
Return & Order Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of being placed, as long as the order has not yet entered processing. To cancel, go to "My Orders" and select "Cancel Order," or contact support with your order number. Once an order has shipped, it can no longer be cancelled — you'll need to use our return process instead once it arrives. Custom or personalized items cannot be cancelled once production has started, even within the 1-hour window.
Return & Payment Methods & Billing
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payment is charged in full at the time of purchase, not at shipment. If your card is declined, your order will not be processed and you'll receive an email explaining the issue — no partial charges are made. For orders over $200, we may place a temporary authorization hold to verify the card before completing the charge. We do not store full card numbers on our servers; all payments are processed through a PCI-compliant third-party provider. Sales tax is calculated automatically at checkout based on your shipping address.
Return & Product Warranty
All products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. The warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, unauthorized repairs, or normal wear and tear. To make a warranty claim, contact support with your order number, a description of the issue, and photos of the defect. Approved claims are resolved with a free repair, replacement, or store credit, at our discretion. The warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser. Extended warranty plans purchased separately are covered under a different policy — contact support for details.
